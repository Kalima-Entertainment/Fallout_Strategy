BECOME A LEGEND

On Fallout Strategy you are in a godlike position where you have full control of newborn colony founded in the wasteland, you have to manage different resources and troops to protect your colony from the foreigns colonies or deathly Deathclaws. If you feel with power enough you can also try to conquer the other bases to expand your borders and become the biggest power in the Commonwealth.
