ABOUT THE GAME

BECOME A LEGEND

On Fallout Strategy you are in a godlike position where you have full control of newborn colony founded in the wasteland, you have to manage different resources and troops to protect your colony from the foreigns colonies or deathly Deathclaws. If you feel with power enough you can also try to conquer the other bases to expand your borders and become the biggest power in the Commonwealth.

CONQUEST THE WASTELAND

Build your community, create units that will help on your journey. Collect different resources to power your colony, and boost it by upgrading your buildings. Make your quest easier while listening the best hits on the radio. But don't be to cocky, every game the map will be different, so you won't be able to use the same strategy.

MEET YOUR FOES

Choose your faction among 4 different classes, create your defenses or be the first to attack. Choose Vault if you want a more moderate experience; Supermutants if "Killer" is your middle name; Brotherhood to be the most tankier around the corner ; or Ghouls if you want a more dynamic experience. But remember, be the last survivor.

BE AWARE OF THE MENACE

The Deathclaw is genetically-engineered creatures developed by the United States military to replace humans during close-combat search-and-destroy missions. They escaped into the wild in the aftermath of the Great War,and you must be ready to deal with them at the end of the game.

TRAILER

Now is your turn: Create a faction, Survive in the Wasteland and Beat the Deathclaws. And remember:

War, War never changes.

THE TEAM : KALIMA ENTERTAINMENT

Kalima Entertainment is an indie video game company based in Terrassa, Barcelona. It is formed by a group of students from the second year of the Bachelor's degree in Video Game Design and Development. Our goal is to make an enjoyable experience in a real-time strategy game with resource management.
